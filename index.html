Biosketch

Xinyi Yang is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Biostatistics and Informatics at Colorado School of Public Health, University of Colorado Denver | Anschutz Medical Campus. She received her MSPH in Biostatistics at Emory University, and B.S. in Applied Mathematics and B.S. in Biological Technology at Nankai University. Her research focuses on biomedical image computing and deep learning with Dr. Fuyong Xing.
